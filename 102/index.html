How To Change Your Oil

Step 1.

First start by parking your vehicle on a flat, level surface.

Step 2.

Next set your parking brake and chock your wheels. This will keep the vehicle from rolling away while your working on it.

Step 3.

Then jack up the front of the vehicle in manufactures designated jack point.

Step 4.

Remove the skid plate.

Step 5.

Remove the engine oil drain plug and catch the used oil in a suitable container.

Step 6.

Install a new drain plug gasket on the drain plug while the oil is draining.

Step 7.

Once the oil slows to a drip every 5-10 seconds, reinstall the drain plug and tighten it to the manufacturures specs.

Step 8.

Remove the oil filter and replace with a new filter, making sure to lubricate the sealing o-ring with oil pior to installation.

Step 9.

Fill your engine with new oil, following your manufacuters stated weight and quantity of oil.
